Now my PhD is done and dusted, I have decided to get back on the blogging horse. My plan is to spend 2 or 3 hours a week blogging. Until now, I have been using the Blogger platform (My old blog is here) but I have been planning for some time on moving to a platform that is more open, more flexible and at the same time quick to set up and post, lightweight whilst also being free.

I had little interest in learning PHP and setting up a Wordpress site and the couple of content management systems I looked at (Joomla and Wordpress.org) seemed to suffer from the same problems that Blogger does. Then I looked at setting up something in Python and Django, but the framework is just so massive that it seemslike overkill to build a relatively simple blog using this.
